What's the right way to put on gear?


click the bag to see the gear and then click the player to see the order

According to many coaches, gear should be put on in this order:

  • guard
  • shin guards
  • socks
  • pants
  • skates
  • shoulder pads
  • elbow pads
  • jersey
  • mouth guard
  • helmet
  • gloves
